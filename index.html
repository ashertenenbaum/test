    About Saint Matthews

    History of the Church

    St Matthew-in-the-City, is one of the most renown and grand churches in New Zealand. The church was designed with a neo-Gothic architecture style and was opened in 1905. It is the only church in the New Zealand with vaulted stone ceilings. 

     

    Saint Matthews is known for its inclusive approach and engagement with city life, offering a venue for dinners, concerts and more. The church provides a spiritual place where people can connect and seek common ground, with services such as the weekly Eucharist, Sermons, and Digital Library and Archives. The church's commitment, important values and community involvement makes it a unique and memorable place of worship.
